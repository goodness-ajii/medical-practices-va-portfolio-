> **Disclaimer:** This checklist reflects general best practices for HIPAA compliance in a virtual healthcare support role. It does not constitute legal advice. ## Why This Matters

As a Virtual Receptionist working with Protected Health Information (PHI), I am classified as a **Business Associate** under the HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules. This means I am legally responsible for safeguarding patient information at the same standard as the practice itself.

A single HIPAA violation can result in:

- Civil penalties from $100 to $50,000+ per violation
- Reputational damage to the practice
- Loss of patient trust
- Termination of contract
- In severe cases, criminal liability

This document is my proactive commitment to operating at the highest standard of patient privacy.

## 6. Patient Identity Verification Protocol

### Standard verification (most calls)

Before discussing any health information, confirm **two** of the following:

- ☐ Full legal name
- ☐ Date of birth
- ☐ Phone number on file
- ☐ Email on file
- ☐ Last appointment date

### Heightened verification (sensitive requests)

For requests involving sensitive PHI release, billing changes, or third-party disclosure, confirm **three** identifiers AND check for a written authorization on file.

### What to do if verification fails

> "I'm not able to confirm that information over the phone right now, but I'd be happy to send a secure message through the Client Portal so we can help you safely. Would that work?"

**Do not:**
- Share any PHI based on caller's claims alone
- Confirm that someone is even a patient at the practice
- Take the caller's word for granted on identity

## 7. Voicemail HIPAA Standards

A voicemail can be heard by anyone in earshot of the patient's phone — including family, roommates, employers, or strangers. Treat every voicemail as **public**.

### Allowed in a voicemail
- ✅ My first name
- ✅ Practice name
- ✅ A generic callback request
- ✅ Practice phone number

### Never in a voicemail
- ❌ Treatment names ("calling about your Botox")
- ❌ Provider names tied to clinical detail
- ❌ Lab/test results
- ❌ Billing balances or financial details
- ❌ Appointment specifics ("your filler at 2 PM")
- ❌ Anything that confirms the listener is receiving a specific service

### Approved template
> "Hi [First Name], this is Goodness from Glow Aesthetics. Please give us a call back at (305) 555-0140 at your convenience. Thank you and have a beautiful day."

## 8. Incident Response

### What counts as a HIPAA incident

- A non-authorized person overhears a call
- PHI is sent to the wrong recipient (email, fax, SMS)
- A device with potential PHI access is lost or stolen
- A non-secure tool is accidentally used (e.g., personal Gmail to send patient info)
- Suspicious account activity (unauthorized login attempts)
- A caller successfully gets information without proper verification

### My response protocol

1. **Stop the spread** — recall email, retract message, lock device, etc.
2. **Document the facts** — what happened, when, who was involved, what PHI may have been exposed.
3. **Notify Office Manager Kevin within 1 hour** via Slack DM + phone call.
4. **Submit written incident report** within 24 hours using the practice's incident form.
5. **Cooperate fully** with the practice's HIPAA officer for any investigation.
6. **Apply the lesson** — update my checklist or workflow to prevent recurrence.

> Reporting an incident is not a punishment — it's the policy. Concealment is the violation.

## 9. Onboarding Compliance Checklist

Before my first shift with a new practice, the following must be complete:

- ☐ Signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
- ☐ Signed confidentiality / non-disclosure agreement
- ☐ Reviewed practice's Notice of Privacy Practices
- ☐ HIPAA training certificate on file (annual renewal)
- ☐ Workspace setup confirmed compliant
- ☐ Device security audit completed
- ☐ Practice login credentials issued (no shared credentials)
- ☐ MFA enabled on all practice accounts
- ☐ Emergency contact / escalation chain documented
- ☐ Incident reporting form template saved
