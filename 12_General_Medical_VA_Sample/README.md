## About This Companion Project

This folder is a **second portfolio sample** — separate from the MedSpa Virtual Receptionist Operations Kit. Where that primary kit demonstrates niche aesthetics expertise, **this sample demonstrates the broader skill set required for general medical practice virtual assistants**:

- Data entry into EHR / practice management systems
- Email communication with patients and providers
- Appointment scheduling and management
- Medical terminology fluency
- HIPAA-compliant administrative operations
- Daily workflow structure

It was built specifically to show range: medspa specialty + general medical VA capability.
