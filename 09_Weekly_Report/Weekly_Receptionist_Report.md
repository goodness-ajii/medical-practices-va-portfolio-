### 7.2 Friction patterns observed

- **Thursday 7 PM slot:** Booked solid all month; consider opening a second late evening (e.g., Tuesday 6–7 PM) to absorb working professional demand.
- **Saturday morning:** Patients consistently arriving early; consider 9:30 AM start instead of 10:00 AM.
- **Voicemail spike:** Most voicemails come in between 5:30–7:00 PM (after-hours). Voicemail greeting could mention online booking and Client Portal to reduce return-call volume.

### 7.3 Patient sentiment notes

## 8. Recommendations

Three concrete suggestions for the practice this week:

1. **Bundle Botox + Lip Filler as a packaged consultation.** 8 callers asked about this combo in one week — there's clear demand. A simple "Refresh Combo Consult" offering could increase same-day treatment conversion.

2. **Update the after-hours voicemail greeting.** Add: *"For faster service, you can also book through our Client Portal at glowaestheticsmiami.com/portal."* Could reduce voicemail return-call workload by ~30%.

3. **Open a second late-evening slot.** Thursday 7 PM is consistently overbooked; a Tuesday 6–7 PM slot could absorb working professional demand without straining staffing.
