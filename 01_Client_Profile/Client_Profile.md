## 6. Patient Persona Snapshots

Understanding the typical caller helps the VA personalize each interaction.

### Persona A — "Curious First-Timer"
- Female, 28–40, professional
- Calls asking about Botox pricing or "preventative" treatments
- Needs reassurance and education
- Highly likely to book a consult if handled warmly

### Persona B — "Loyal Regular"
- Female, 35–55, returns every 3–4 months
- Calls to book, reschedule, or ask about new services
- Wants speed and recognition ("Hi Mrs. Davis, great to hear from you")

### Persona C — "Event-Driven Booker"
- Any age; has a wedding, reunion, or photo shoot
- Calls with a deadline in mind
- Needs realistic guidance on timing (e.g., "Botox needs ~2 weeks to settle")

### Persona D — "Concerned Post-Treatment Patient"
- Existing patient calling about swelling, bruising, or unexpected results
- **HIGH PRIORITY** — always escalate to a nurse promptly
- Reassurance + immediate action required

## 7. Brand Voice & Communication Style

The phone is the patient's **first touchpoint** with the brand. Glow Aesthetics' voice should always feel:

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Warm, calm, unhurried | Rushed or robotic |
| Confident and informed | Pushy or sales-y |
| Inclusive (use the patient's name) | Use jargon without explanation |
| Professional but personable | Overly casual ("hey," "yeah," "no worries") |
| Educational | Diagnostic ("nurses can advise on that at your visit") |

**Signature greeting (inbound):**
> "Thank you for calling Glow Aesthetics, this is [Name]. How may I help you today?"

**Signature sign-off:**
> "Thank you so much for calling Glow Aesthetics — we look forward to seeing you on [date]. Have a beautiful day."

## 8. Compliance & Privacy Standards

- **HIPAA-compliant workspace** required (private room, locked devices, headset)
- **Business Associate Agreement (BAA)** signed with practice
- **No PHI** ever shared via personal email, SMS, or non-secure messaging
- **Patient identity verification** required before discussing any health information (verify full name + date of birth + one additional identifier)
- **Minimum necessary information** principle followed on every call
- **Incident reporting** — any suspected breach reported to Office Manager within 24 hours
