## Why These Three Scenarios

**New patient inquiry** is the highest-revenue moment a medspa receptionist handles — every booked consultation is potential lifetime patient value.

**Appointment confirmation** is where pre-care compliance happens — a brief mention of "avoid NSAIDs and alcohol 24 hours prior" prevents bruising and complaints.

**Concerned post-treatment caller** is the highest-risk moment — a calm voice that recognizes a vascular concern, verifies identity, and routes to a nurse within 15 minutes is what separates a real medical VA from a generic phone answerer.
